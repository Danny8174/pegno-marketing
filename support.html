Complaints & disputes

Formal Complaints

If you have a complaint about Pegno's service, please email complaints@pegno.app with your name, account email, deal reference (if applicable), and a description of your complaint.

We will acknowledge your complaint within 3 business days and aim to resolve it within 15 business days. If we cannot resolve it within 8 weeks, you may be eligible to refer it to the Financial Ombudsman Service.
